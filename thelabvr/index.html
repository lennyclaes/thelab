Unique experience

At The Lab, we aim to create a social gaming experience for you. Play on our high-end Virtual Reality rigs, complete with dedicated gaming peripherals, to experience a true immersive experience. In addition to that we also offer a large Virtual reality library and collection.

What is The Lab - Virtual Reality?

We are the first Virtual Arcade room in Belgium. Since october 2016 we provide VR experience in Kortrijk.

What can you expect?

We provide two Virtual Reality rooms available for reservation at The Lab Kortrijk. Enjoy delicious drinks and food whilst competing with your friends in a virtual reality landscape of your choosing.

Events

In addition to bringing people together through our Virtual Reality Hall, we aim to give the same experience for events, team building, etc. For your own event, contact us at info@thelab.be or visit our event website Nucleus at thelab.be/nucleus.
